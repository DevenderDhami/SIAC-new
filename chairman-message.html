Chairman's Message

Education is essential for the growth and development of a country, but skill-specific education adds to the overall socio-economic and human resource development of the nation.

We have been working to empower youth through various skill training programs aligned with NSQF/SSC since 2008, contributing toward ‘Viksit Bharat’ and ‘Akhand Bharat’, fulfilling the vision of our Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi to make India the ‘Skill Capital of the World’.

With affiliations across Indian government bodies such as MoRD, UPSDM, PBSSD, MoMA, RSLDC, GNULM, and international organizations, we’ve earned 11 awards including 2 international honors. We are proud to bridge the gap between industry needs and youth skills.

Skilling keeps the wheels of development turning. We focus on strategic operations to deliver effective services and help youth realize their true potential.

Our journey continues with Sikkim Global Technical University (SGTU), envisioned to shape future-ready leaders rooted in integrity, ethics, and responsibility—contributing to SDG 4 and the mission of Viksit & Akhand Bharat.

Let us use our knowledge and skills for the betterment of society. Together, we can build a more progressive and sustainable world for the generations to come.

Dr. Saurabh Singhal
Chairman - IGCSM
