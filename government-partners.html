Our Government Partners

SIAC is proud to collaborate with India’s foremost government agencies and institutions to advance the nation's mission of skill empowerment and inclusive development. Our strategic partnerships span ministries, national councils, and certifying bodies, enabling us to deliver training and assessments that are nationally recognized and industry-relevant. These collaborations not only validate the authenticity and credibility of our programs but also open up wider career opportunities for learners across urban and rural regions.

Through these government affiliations, SIAC contributes to flagship initiatives such as Skill India, Digital India, and Atmanirbhar Bharat. Our programs are designed to meet current market demands and evolving industry standards while ensuring compliance with regulatory frameworks. From youth and women empowerment to entrepreneurship development and employment facilitation, our joint efforts aim to build a future-ready workforce for the nation.

Benefits of Our Government Collaborations:
  • check_circleNational and international recognition of certifications.
  • check_circleAccess to government-funded skill development schemes.
  • check_circleIndustry-relevant curriculum and updated assessment frameworks.
  • check_circleImproved employability and job placement support for students.
  • check_circleParticipation in government-approved events, drives, and upskilling programs.
