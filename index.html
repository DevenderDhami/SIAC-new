About

Skill Initiatives and Assessment Council (SIAC) is committed to empowering youth through practical, industry-aligned skill development programs. Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional education and the dynamic needs of the modern workforce.

At SIAC, we offer hands-on training, collaborate with industry leaders, and implement innovative learning solutions to ensure our students are job-ready and future-focused. Whether you're aiming for a professional career or entrepreneurial journey, SIAC is here to guide and support your growth.

Together, let’s build a skilled, self-reliant India—where every individual has the opportunity to succeed through skill-based education.

Popular Courses

Beauty and Makeup Essentials

Learn the art of beauty and makeup techniques that are perfect for weddings, festivals, and local events. This course will help you start a career as a makeup artist in your community.

Fashion Styling and Personal Grooming

This course teaches you how to style clothes for different occasions like weddings, festivals, and family gatherings. Learn how to help people look and feel their best with fashion and personal grooming skills.

Fashion Designing (Traditional & Modern)

Learn how to design clothes and create fashion for local occasions like weddings, festivals, and day-to-day wear. This course helps you understand fabric, color, and styling to create beautiful and marketable designs.
