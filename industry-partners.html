Our Industry Partners

SIAC is proud to collaborate with leading industry players, corporate organizations, and sector-specific experts to bridge the gap between education and employability. These partnerships play a vital role in shaping skill development programs that are practical, job-oriented, and aligned with real-world workforce requirements. Together, we ensure that learners are equipped with the latest tools, techniques, and technologies relevant to their chosen fields.

Our industry collaborations help co-develop curriculum, conduct hands-on training sessions, and provide internship and placement opportunities across various sectors. By integrating industry insights and standards into our training ecosystem, SIAC empowers youth to become job-ready professionals. These alliances also contribute to workforce upskilling, entrepreneurship development, and innovation in vocational education. With our partners, we are building a stronger, more skilled India—one career at a time.
