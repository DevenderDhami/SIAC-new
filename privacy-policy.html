Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Skill Initiative and Assessment Council (SIAC). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

a. Personal Information
  • Full Name
  • Contact Details (Phone number, Email address)
  • Educational Qualifications
  • Identity Proofs (as required for verification)
b. Non-Personal Information
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Pages visited and usage patterns

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process assessments and skill certifications
  • To maintain communication regarding courses or services
  • To enhance our website and services
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. Your data may be shared with:

  • Authorized training partners
  • Regulatory bodies (if required)
  • Technology providers for data processing

4. Data Security

We implement standard security measures to protect your data. However, no online transmission is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. Please contact us for such requests.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
