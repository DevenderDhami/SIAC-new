About SIAC

Skill Initiatives and Assessment Council (SIAC) is a non-governmental organization established on 28th December 2018, with its headquarters located in New Delhi. The organization is actively involved in the field of education and skill development, focusing on empowering youth through various training and capacity-building programs.

SIAC is registered under the Indian Companies Act with the Corporate Identification Number (CIN): U80902DL2018NPL339732. The council continues to operate with the goal of providing quality skill-based education, particularly to economically disadvantaged communities.

What We Do

At SIAC, we aim to bridge the gap between education and employment through impactful training and development programs.

Skill Development

Offering job-ready courses and hands-on training in various vocational fields for rural and urban youth.

Employment Assistance

Connecting trained candidates with industry partners through placement drives and job fairs.

Entrepreneurship Support

Empowering self-employment through business training, mentoring, and startup support for youth and women.
